How to find the recruiter's contact details for this role:

1. LinkedIn: Search the company name + "Talent Acquisition" or "Recruiter" — filter by location. Send a connection request with a note referencing the job posting.

2. Company careers page: Many list the hiring manager's name in the JD. Google "company name + recruiter + Power BI" to find profiles.

3. Hunter.io: Enter the company domain to find email patterns (e.g. firstname.lastname@company.com).

4. Apollo.io: Free tier lets you find professional emails by name + company.

5. Email pattern guess: Most enterprise companies use firstname.lastname@ or f.lastname@ — try the outreach email tab draft with the pattern once you have the name.
Pro tip for H1B transfers: When you find the recruiter, lead with: "I'm currently on H1B at TCS — this would be a transfer, not a new petition. No lottery required." This removes the biggest blocker before they even ask.
